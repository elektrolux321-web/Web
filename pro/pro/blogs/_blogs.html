understanding asyne/Await with real-life examples

▶

Async/Await Real-Life Example: Ordering Food at a Restaurant .

Imagine you're at a restaurant, and you order food. The process can be broken down like this:

1. You order food (async operation):

You place an order, and the chef starts preparing it. You don't wait at the counter you go back to your table.

2. Chef prepares food (await):

The chef takes time to prepare your food. Meanwhile, you can do other things.

3. Food is ready (result):

When the food is ready, the chef call you, and you collect it.
